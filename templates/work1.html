001 : Datamart as a Service

Description: Datamart as a Service is a comprehensive solution implemented across three critical sections:

  1. Risk: Aggregating customer credit and debit status data to inform credit decisions.
  2. Insurance Products Selling: Collating data from insurance agencies to facilitate passive invoicing for daily/monthly insurance product sales.
  3. Impact of Social Merit Loans/Credit Initiatives: Gathering loan debit data to assess the overall exposure impact.

My Role (Team Leadership): Led functional analysis and described business processes. Managed delivery, system testing, and user feedback.

My Activities:

  • Designed maintenance and End-to-End enhancements.
  • Utilized the following layers:
    • Business Layer: Developed using Oracle 12, incorporating PL/SQL for business logic, calculations, data integrity checks, BI star schema, and output data files.
    • Reporting Layer: Leveraged Power BI/Tabular for analytic reporting.
    • Front END Layer: Implemented Java SE over GIT and Spring framework.
  • Extracted data from various sources using ETL IBM Datastage (v. 11.3, 11.5).
